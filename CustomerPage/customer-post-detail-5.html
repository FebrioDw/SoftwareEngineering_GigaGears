Which Smartwatch Offers the Best Value for Fitness Tracking?

FitnessTechie • 1 week ago
980 Views
180 Likes
15 comments

I’ve been thinking about getting a smartwatch to track my workouts and overall fitness, but I’m torn between the Apple Watch Series 8, the Garmin Forerunner 955, and the Samsung Galaxy Watch 5. Each seems to have its strengths, but I’m looking for something that offers the best value in terms of fitness features. Any recommendations from those who have used these watches?

Discussion

FitFanatic2025

I’ve used both the Apple Watch Series 8 and the Samsung Galaxy Watch 5. For fitness tracking, I’d give the edge to the Garmin Forerunner 955. It’s built specifically for athletes with advanced metrics like heart rate variability, VO2 max, and recovery tracking. It’s also super comfortable for long workouts.

TechLover23

If you're looking for a well-rounded smartwatch that also offers fitness tracking, the Apple Watch Series 8 is a great choice. The fitness features are solid, and I love the seamless integration with the iPhone. Plus, it has a more stylish design if you’re into wearing your smartwatch as a fashion accessory too.
